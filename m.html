Wet n Joy Water Park

India's Biggest Destination for Unlimited Water Fun

Wet n Joy Water Park is one of the most popular water parks in India,
located in Lonavala and Shirdi. It offers thrilling water rides, wave pools, rain dance, lazy rivers, and fun zones for all age groups.
Wet n Joy is known for its world-class rides, safety standards, and joyful environment that makes every visit memorable.
